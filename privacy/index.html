2. Information we collect

We collect only the information you submit through the forms on this website and technical context attached to the submission:

For the demo access form, a region may be inferred from your browser locale. This is not a verified physical location, and we do not perform IP geolocation.

3. Purpose and legal basis

We process this information to respond to your request: a Cloud application, a Self-Hosted quote request, or demo access. The legal basis is your consent, given through the consent checkbox on each form, and our legitimate interest in responding to business inquiries that you initiate.

Form submissions are delivered by FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form-forwarding service, to our contact mailbox. FormSubmit processes the submitted data solely to deliver it to us.

5. Retention

We keep form submissions for as long as needed to handle your request and any resulting commercial discussion, and afterwards only as required for legitimate business records or legal obligations.

6. Sharing and international transfers

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by the form delivery provider and our email provider, which may involve transfers outside the EEA subject to appropriate safeguards under applicable law.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, restrict or object to processing, request portability, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact prodmic@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority; in Portugal this is the CNPD.

We may update this policy from time to time. The date above reflects the latest revision.
