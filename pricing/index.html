A managed option with a $0 monthly fee and a usage fee of 0.5% of successful deposit volume routed through Smart Cashier. Commercial terms depend on traffic, scope, hosting, and support requirements. See pricing details.

The Cloud option in detail.

Cloud is a secondary, managed option: $0 monthly fee, no setup fee, no integration fee, no minimum commitment, and no source-code access.

The usage fee is 0.5% of the USD value of successful deposits routed through Smart Cashier. Failed deposits are not included.

Infrastructure, PSP, acquiring, and third-party fees are separate and remain the customer's responsibility. Final definitions, reporting, exclusions, and invoicing are documented in the commercial agreement.
