Licensing notes: the Self-Hosted license is non-exclusive and Smart Cashier may be licensed to multiple operators. Use by additional companies in the same holding, resale, sublicensing, and external commercialization require written agreement or approval. How Cloud pricing is calculated.

The fee is calculated as 0.5% of the USD value of successful deposits routed through Smart Cashier. Failed deposits are not included in successful deposit volume.

Infrastructure, PSP, acquiring, and third-party fees are separate and remain the customer's responsibility.

Final definitions, reporting, exclusions, invoicing, and settlement are documented in the commercial agreement.
