Self-hosted payment orchestration for iGaming

Self-hosted payment routing with full source code.

Some operators cannot, or do not want to, send payment traffic through a vendor's cloud. Smart Cashier puts the entire routing layer on infrastructure you control: full source code, modification rights, and deployment without vendor access to payment data.

View the demo

Who self-hosting is for.

Operators with strict data-control requirements, holdings that want the routing layer inside their own perimeter, platforms that need to embed orchestration into their product, and teams that want to own their critical payment infrastructure.

The license is perpetual and non-exclusive, with internal-use and modification rights. Use across additional holding companies, resale, and sublicensing require written agreement.

How the engagement works.

The path starts with a controlled pilot on your payment data: assessment, historical replay, shadow routing, and, where appropriate, limited live traffic with agreed success criteria.

After validation, the delivery can include the source code, documentation, deployment scripts, integration support, architecture review, team training, updates under the agreement, and priority support. Deployment targets include on-premise and private cloud.

Common questions.

Is the license exclusive?

No. The license is non-exclusive, and Smart Cashier may be licensed to multiple operators.

Can we modify the code?

Yes. The license includes internal-use and modification rights for the licensed operator.

Does the vendor see our payment data?

Self-hosted deployment is designed to run entirely on your infrastructure, without vendor access to payment data.
