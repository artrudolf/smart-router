Payment routing for online casinos.

Casino deposits fail for reasons a static setup cannot see: an issuer that dislikes one acquirer, a PSP degrading in one market, a method that underperforms at night. Smart Cashier routes each eligible deposit to the provider most likely to approve it, and lets you validate the model on your own data first.

Why casino deposits fail more than they should.

Most casinos send every deposit down the same path regardless of context. But approval performance differs by country, card BIN, issuer, payment method, and time of day. A route that approves 90% of one segment may approve far less of another.

When routing is managed manually, the payment team reacts after the damage: a provider degrades, approval drops, players leave the cashier, and the decline report explains it days later.

How Smart Cashier addresses it.

Smart Cashier sits between your cashier and your payment service providers (PSPs). For each eligible deposit it compares provider availability, approval performance for the last 60 minutes in the relevant segment, cost, and your rules, then selects the strongest route.

The model is configured around your historical payment data and validated through a controlled pilot: data assessment, historical replay, shadow routing, and, where appropriate, limited live traffic. After validation, the routing layer can run on your own infrastructure under a source-code license.

Common questions.

Does this replace our casino's PSPs?

No. Smart Cashier routes across the PSPs you already use. It is orchestration software, not a payment provider.

Can routing differ per market?

Yes. Segment-based routing allows different logic per country, currency, BIN range, method, device, and more.

Do we have to commit before seeing results?

No. The commercial path starts with a controlled pilot scoped around your data, with success criteria agreed up front.
