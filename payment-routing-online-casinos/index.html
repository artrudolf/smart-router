Payment orchestration for iGaming

Payment routing for online casinos.

Casino deposits fail for reasons a static setup cannot see: an issuer that dislikes one acquirer, a PSP degrading in one market, a method that underperforms at night. Smart Cashier is designed to route each eligible deposit to the provider most likely to approve it.

Why casino deposits fail more than they should.

Most casinos send every deposit down the same path regardless of context. But approval performance differs by country, card BIN, issuer, payment method, and time of day. A route that approves 90% of Portuguese bank transfers may approve far less of the same traffic from another market.

When routing is managed manually, the payment team reacts after the damage: a provider degrades, approval drops, players leave the cashier, and the decline report explains it days later.

How Smart Cashier addresses it.

Smart Cashier sits between your cashier and your payment service providers (PSPs). For each eligible deposit it compares provider availability, recent approval performance for the relevant segment, cost, and your rules, then selects the strongest route.

Live health monitoring can shift traffic away from a degrading provider automatically, and controlled cascading can retry eligible soft declines through another approved route with duplicate-charge protection. Your team keeps manual rules, priorities, and override.

Common questions.

Does this replace our casino's PSPs?

No. Smart Cashier routes across the PSPs you already use. It is orchestration software, not a payment provider.

Can routing differ per market?

Yes. Segment-based routing allows different logic per country, currency, BIN range, method, device, and more.

Is the product live in production?

The current public version is a working product demo. Production integrations are scoped around each operator's stack.
